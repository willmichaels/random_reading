# Random Technical Wiki

A web app that fetches random Vital Wikipedia articles by category and lets you download them as plain text or PDF. Log in to sync your article log across devices.

## Features

- **Random article discovery** — Browse Wikipedia's Vital articles by category and get a random article from your selection.
- **Download options** — Save articles as plain text or PDF for offline reading.
- **Log** — Track which articles you've read. When logged in, your log syncs across devices.
- **My Links** — Save and organize Wikipedia links with notes for later reading.
